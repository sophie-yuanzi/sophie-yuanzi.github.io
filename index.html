Yuan Zi

Assistant Professor of Economics

Geneva Graduate Institute (IHEID)



I am an Assistant Professor in Economics at the Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies (IHEID).

I am also a research affiliate at University of Oslo and the Centre for Economic and Policy Research (CEPR). My research interests include international trade, economic geography and economic development.
